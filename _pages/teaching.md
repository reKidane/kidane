## Mentoring And Training

My teaching and mentoring work is rooted in experimental design, field and controlled-environment phenotyping, plant physiology measurements, metabolomics workflows, and reproducible data analysis. I have mentored students, technicians, junior researchers, and collaborative project teams across crop physiology, plant breeding, and molecular phenotyping activities.

## Training Areas

- **Crop physiology and phenotyping:** gas exchange, chlorophyll fluorescence, water status, above- and belowground trait measurement, and phenology scoring.
- **Metabolomics and ionomics:** sample preparation, GC-MS/LC-MS and ICP-OES workflows, metabolite identification, spectral curation, quantification, and interpretation.
- **Root and shoot trait analysis:** root system architecture, rootstock-mediated traits, ion partitioning, and root-shoot integration under abiotic stress.
- **Data analysis and modeling:** R, Python, multivariate analysis, machine learning, network analysis, genotype-by-environment analysis, and trait-performance modeling.
- **Crop improvement:** field trial design, hybrid development, heterosis and combining ability analysis, germplasm characterization, and variety development pipelines.
