Kidanemaryam Wagaw Reta is a plant physiologist and plant biochemist whose research integrates crop physiology, plant metabolomics, root-shoot phenotyping, multi-omics analysis, and predictive modeling to understand crop resilience under abiotic stress.

He is currently a postdoctoral researcher in plant metabolomics and physiology at Ben-Gurion University of the Negev, where he leads work on grapevine heatwave responses, UAV thermal sensing, metabolomics, gene expression, and AI-assisted performance forecasting.
