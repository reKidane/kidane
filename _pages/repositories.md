## Analysis Resources

- **Physiology and phenotyping workflows:** gas exchange, chlorophyll fluorescence, UAV thermal sensing, and root-shoot trait measurements.
- **Metabolomics and ionomics workflows:** GC-MS, LC-MS, HS-SPME-GC-MS, ICP-OES, metabolite annotation, spectral curation, and pathway interpretation.
- **Modeling workflows:** R and Python analysis for multivariate statistics, network inference, machine learning, feature selection, and genotype-by-environment analysis.
- **Research operations:** electronic lab notebooks, SOPs, technical reports, experimental design templates, and reproducible data-management practices.
